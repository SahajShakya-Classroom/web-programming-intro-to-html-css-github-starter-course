The Ultimate Driving Machine

BMW (Bayerische Motoren Werke AG), or Bavarian Motor Works, is one of the world's leading manufacturers of luxury automobiles and motorcycles. Headquartered in Munich, Germany, the brand is famously associated with its slogan, "The Ultimate Driving Machine."

The BMW M5 is essentially the benchmark for the "super sedan" category. For 40 years, it has been the gold standard for a car that can comfortably drive you to a business meeting in a suit and then destroy a race track on the way home.
